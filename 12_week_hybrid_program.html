Markow Key Cues

Towel Setup

  • Catch top of pelvis and nipple line of ribcage
  • Use bigger towel than shown in videos
  • Position matters — game-changer for pelvis shape

Rolling For Hip Rotation

  • Keep weight light — stay relaxed
  • Roll right to left first (for left hip IR)
  • Add hooklying before if very limited

Split Squat Isometric

  • Heel heavy throughout
  • Belt buckle away from stuck hip
  • 1st and 5th metatarsal down in back

Hip IR Kinstretch

  • Setup 10-15° beyond active ROM
  • PAILs = external rotation driving down
  • RAILs = internal rotation lifting foot
  • Cramping = try again

Readiness-Based Swaps

🛌 Low Sleep (3-4 hours)

  • Drop plyometrics entirely
  • Drop PZ Max → do Z2 instead
  • Stay at RPE 6-7 max for lifts
  • Keep mobility — it's restorative
  • Skip Farmer's walks

😓 High Fatigue

  • Use home (banded) versions of exercises
  • Skip Farmer's walks
  • Keep core and mobility
  • Drop last 2-3 accessories if needed

🤒 Sick

  • Mobility + CARs only
  • Skip all strength
  • Skip all conditioning
  • Light GMB acceptable

✈️ Travel

  • Hip CARs daily (5-10 min)
  • Bodyweight squats, lunges, push-ups
  • Bands for hip abduction
  • Markow videos on phone
  • Evening Markow menu is ideal

⏱️ Short on Time

  • Cut: Farmer's → Plyos → Last accessories → PZ length
  • Never cut: Main lifts, Markow, Core
  • GMB can shorten to 8-10 min

🔥 Feeling Great

  • Push to top of RPE range
  • Add 1 set to main lifts
  • Extend PZ by 10-15 min
  • Don't go crazy — bank the win

  • Or: Accept the miss, Day 1 only for lower this week

Missed Thursday

  • Combine with Friday (shorten both)
  • Or: Saturday AM before conditioning

Missed Friday

Overlay Deload Structure (When Triggered)

  • Strength: Full volume as programmed (no deload)
  • Z2: 1 × 60 min only
  • PZ/PZ Max: Skip or halve duration
  • Mobility: Full volume (restorative)
  • Core: Full volume
  • Sprint prep / Plyos: Skip
  • Farmer's walks: Skip

Red Line Rule

🚨 KICKBOXING: If you feel low back compensation during kicks → STOP and do hip mobility instead. Radiculopathy is the red line. No exceptions.
