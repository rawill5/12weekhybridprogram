Program Philosophy

This program integrates strength, conditioning, mobility, power, and skill work using a Norton PHAT strength base with Markow hip restructuring, Luke Thomas kick-specific work, and Galpin/Attia conditioning principles.

🎯 Primary Goal

Chambered roundhouse kick at nose height, 3-second hold, no lumbar compensation or pelvic tilt.

🦴 Key Limitation

Hip internal rotation + abduction (hip capsule pattern). Left hip IR is the primary bottleneck.

⚠️ Constraint

Lumbar radiculopathy — box squat and trap bar DL cleared. Manage cumulative spinal compression.
